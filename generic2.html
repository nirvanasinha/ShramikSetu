Manpower Services

Equipped with a well-maintained database of resumes and possessing a team of high-end professionals, we have carved a niche for ourselves in the field with our recruitment services. Efficient manpower is the key to success for every organization. We stand for providing impeccable manpower services for our clients within the stipulated time frame. Seeing the tremendous boom in the industrial sector, the demand for manpower is increasing day by day. So far, we have catered to the needs of a number of industries including retail warehousing, various supply chain services, engineering, petrochemical, health care, IT, hospitality, real estate, banking and finance, electrical engineering, outsourcing industries, etc.

We follow a unique methodology for the selection of the candidates which includes their proper screening on the basis of aptitude and skill. At the same time, we also ensure that the candidate is medically fit for the job. We keep the specific requisites of the clients in mind before selecting the candidates.

Recruitment Process


Our unique methodology for the recruitment of the right professional for the right job profile comprises of the following stages:
Close encounter with the clients to determine their exact requisites for the job profile
Browsing through our all-embracing database of candidates for the selection of the right match
Screening the candidates on the basis of skills, behavior and on other parameters
Proper medical examination of the candidates at authorized centers
Timely accomplishment of all the recruitment procedures by following protocols
Handling ticketing along with other travel documents so that the candidates reach the other end comfortably and on time
Our commitment to quality services s the outcome of the stringent process of recruitment carried out by a highly experienced and expert team. The efforts of the Management team to play a significant role in achieving this goal.
